About Us

We are the Scarlett Spacehawks.
Our team represents the Illinois Institute of Technology in the NASA Robot Mining Competition at the Kennedy Space Center.Apart from participating in NASA's competition, our mission is to actively reach out to the youth, and inspire the minds of our future STEM leaders and innovators.

"Lofty" 2017

Lofty competed on May 2017 against 47 other universitites. He underwent many upgrades during the competition and the team managed to win 3rd place! in the mining category!

"Dora" 2018

Dora competed on May 2018. She is still in the works and will undergoe many upgrades. She's expected to compete again this coming Competition on May 2019.
